Company Goals

Though the construction industry has yet to fully recover from the pandemic’s impacts, a common theme has re-emerged. Skilled labor is once again in short supply and there is an expectation that the shortage will steadily worsen if construction’s recovery accelerates in the coming months as anticipated. This is where we come in. Skilled workers available now and apprentices able to find jobs for struggling employees. Helping the next generation of tradies start their careers. Our aim is to connect willing workers with employers that are all ready struggling to keep up with demand all around the country

Aims

  • To provide the easiest way to connect wokrers to employers
  • Providing employers with skilled workers immediately
  • Finding potential apprentices with the best job suited to them
  • Profile building for indivuals to track jobs and get paid fast

Tools

  1. Featured App for real time updates
  2. Buisness advertising for growth
  3. Networking through skilled trades
  4. Able to accept jobs instantly
